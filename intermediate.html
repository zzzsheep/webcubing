Intermediate Tutorial

Once you are able to solve the cube under 50 seconds, now learning a new method called CFOP to have a faster time ranging from 50-20 seconds

1.Faster white cross

By finishing the white cross in a shorter amount of time will decrease your overall time significantly. Watch this video to improve your white cross.

2.White cross

1. Find the white center piece, then hold it on top.
2. Find the red center piece and rotate it front of you.
3. Find white/red edge pieces, then line it up with the white center.
4. Do that for the rest three colors.
