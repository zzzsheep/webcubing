Beginner tutorial

This tutoiral is for a complete beginner that dont know anything about solving a rubik's cube

1.Learn the basic

The beginner's method is the easiest method to learn to solve a rubik's cube
First you have to learn the types of pieces that makes up a rubiks cube.
The three types are the center, edge and corner pieces.
There are three layers, first, middle and top.

2.White cross

1. Find the white center piece, then hold it on top.
2. Find the red center piece and rotate it front of you.
3. Find white/red edge pieces, then line it up with the white center.
4. Do that for the rest three colors.
